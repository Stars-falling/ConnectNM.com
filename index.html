ConnectNM

Our mission

Our mission at ConnectNM is to, as the name implies, connect New Mexico across county lines.
By cataloging all of the events in every county across the state we hope people can find things to do in their home city or town, or go and visit a different one and participate.
By making it easier to find places to volunteer, connect and learn within the community, we hope to foster more connections between people and help people be able to actively
participate in their community.

How it works

Every week we'll review and collect any new events and update the website.

Go to counties, you can filter by county and see all of the events happening in the area.
In the same tab, you can find resources for community service, and programs in a certain county.

You can submit events or resources that we missed through the form on the top, any comments are appreciated!
